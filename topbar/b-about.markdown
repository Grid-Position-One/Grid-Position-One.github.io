# About Grid Position One

Grid Position One is an open-source, community-driven blog for Formula 1 enthusiasts. We are a group of F1 fans who came together to share race analysis, technical explainers, and paddock insights.

## Why "Grid Position One"?

In Formula 1, pole position — P1 on the grid — is where every driver wants to start. It represents the perfect lap, the culmination of engineering excellence and driver skill. We chose this name because we believe every F1 fan deserves a front-row seat to understanding and enjoying the sport.

## What We Cover

* **Race Analysis** — Deep dives into race strategy, tyre degradation models, and overtaking data
* **Technical Explainers** — Breaking down aerodynamics, power units, and engineering innovations in plain English
* **Season Tracking** — Championship standings, head-to-head comparisons, and statistical insights
* **Paddock News** — Driver market movements, team developments, and the stories that shape the sport

## Get Involved

Grid Position One is open to contributions. Whether you want to write race recaps, analyze data, or just join the discussion — we'd love to have you on the grid.
